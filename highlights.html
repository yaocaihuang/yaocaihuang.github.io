Ionizing Radiation Acoustic Imaging (iRAI)

iRAI uses a special ultrasound array to “listen” to tiny sound waves created when therapeutic X-rays hit the body. By turning these signals into 3D images fused with CT, it lets doctors see exactly where the radiation dose lands inside the patient. This real-time feedback helps ensure the radiation precisely hits the tumor while sparing healthy organs.
