Yaocai Huang

I am from China and currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Biomedical Engineering at the University of Michigan. I am a member of the Optical Imaging Lab under the supervision of Prof. Xueding Wang.

Google Scholar LinkedIn Email

Research Interests

My research focuses on ionizing radiation acoustic imaging (iRAI) and photoacoustic endoscopy, aiming to improve radiotherapy dose visualization and develop endoscopic tools to assess intestinal diseases. I work across simulations, phantom studies, and first-in-human experiments, with emphasis on algorithm development, system integration, and quantitative validation for clinical translation.
